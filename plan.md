# Professional Online Appearance & Reputation Management Plan

## 🎯 Goal
Maintain a high-quality, professional digital presence while minimizing "dirt" or irrelevant personal information that could influence employers.

---

## 🔍 Phase 1: The "Digital Audit"
Before cleaning, you must know what is out there.
- [ ] **Google Search your name**: Use "Jacob Moreno" + "Austin", "Jacob Moreno" + "Developer", etc.
- [ ] **Incognito Search**: Use a private window to see what a stranger sees.
- [ ] **Search Images**: Check for old party photos or tagged posts.
- [ ] **Check "People Search" sites**: These are the primary sources of "dirt" (home addresses, relatives, old court records).

---

## 🚫 Phase 2: Scrubbing the "Dirt" Sites
These sites aggregate data and can be a liability. You should manually opt-out of each.

### Top Data Brokers to Opt-Out Of:
1. **Spokeo**: [spokeo.com/optout](https://www.spokeo.com/optout)
2. **Whitepages**: [whitepages.com/suppression-requests](https://www.whitepages.com/suppression-requests)
3. **MyLife**: This site often shows "Reputation Scores." Call their support or use their opt-out form.
4. **BeenVerified**: [beenverified.com/app/optout](https://www.beenverified.com/app/optout)
5. **Intelius**: [intelius.com/opt-out](https://www.intelius.com/opt-out)
6. **Radaris**: [radaris.com/control/privacy](https://radaris.com/control/privacy)
